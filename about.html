Here is my photography website as a final project for Front End Web Development course at General Assembly, Autumn 2013. I have chosen Nixie One (thanks Google) as a font for its clean and simple look which doesn't interfere with the images. When you hover over images opacity changes to 0.8, while textshadow is applied when you hover over the navigation bar links. In the about page, clicking on my email opens up a mail window filling in automatically the address and the title. The html and CSS is responsive, always filling the browser window and moving around the navigation bar when screen is smaller than 768px. I have also added a favicon on all pages.

In terms of Javascript, I have used mouse events so when one clicks on an images, that opens up on full screen, which I think works best to look at photos without distractions. To navigate the gallery one can use both mouse clicks and keyboard arrows. The transition between one image and the other is done using fade in/out methods and different styles are applied for landscape/portrait photos automatically.
